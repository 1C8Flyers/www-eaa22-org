---
title: "Aircraft Builds"
---

Chapter 22 supports collaborative **aircraft build** and restoration activity that helps members develop practical skills and deeper systems knowledge.

Typical focus areas include:

- Airframe and systems familiarization
- Safe shop practices and tooling
- Build-planning and project management
- Peer mentoring from experienced builders and maintainers

If you are interested in joining a build effort, contact the chapter through [Contact](/contact/).
