## Course highlights

- In-depth coverage of key topics: aerodynamics, navigation, weather, regulations, and more
- Interactive instruction with practical examples and discussion
- Personalized guidance from experienced chapter instructors
- Study resources that support both class time and independent review
- Networking with fellow local aviators

## Who should attend

- Aspiring private pilots building core aeronautical knowledge
- Aviation enthusiasts wanting a stronger technical foundation
- Learners preparing for the FAA Private Pilot Knowledge Test

Chapter 22 aims to keep aviation education accessible: members may attend at low/no cost, and non-members are welcome with a small donation.
