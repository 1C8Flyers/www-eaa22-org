---
title: "Scout Merit Badges"
---

Chapter 22 periodically supports **aviation merit badge** opportunities for scout groups.

These sessions are designed to be educational, practical, and inspiring for youth participants, and may include:

- Core aviation concepts
- Safety and airport awareness
- Intro to aircraft and flight operations
- Age-appropriate career exploration

If your Scouts woud like to schedule a workshop, please reach out on [Contact](/contact/).
