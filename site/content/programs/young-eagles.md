The **Young Eagles Program** is EAA's flagship youth aviation program and has introduced millions of young people to flight.

Chapter 22 uses Young Eagles to give local youth a positive, first-hand aviation experience in a safe and welcoming environment.

## Who it's for

- Youth ages **8â€“17**
- Families interested in aviation pathways
- Students who want an inspiring first step into flight training

## What participants can expect

- A short preflight/ground orientation
- A flight with a qualified volunteer pilot (typically around 20 minutes)
- Exposure to takeoff, landing, and local-area flight
- Encouragement to continue learning through aviation education resources

Young Eagles flights are provided through volunteer support and chapter participation.
